How to play

  • Navigate the field with arrows:
    • ⬆️: Move forward
    • ⬇️: Move backward
    • ⬅️: Move left
    • ➡️: Move right
  • Be brave and ruthless! Survival of the universe is at stake.
  • Press `space` to shoot and defeat all enemies.
  • You start with three lives & lose a life if hit multiple times. Game over if you loos 3 lives.
  • Leave no enemy alive to save a hero and earn a trophy card.
  • Advance to the next level after each victory.
