How to Play

  • Navigate the field with arrows:
    • : Move Down
    • : Move Up
    • : Move Backward
    • : Move Forward

  • Press `SPACE` to shoot and defeat the enemies.
  • Be brave and ruthless! Survival of the universe is at stake.
  • You will get wounded in battle. Monitor your lifespan on the right: the bar decreases each time you're hit.
  • Kill the boss to save the heroes and earn a reward.
  • Advance to the next level after each victory.
